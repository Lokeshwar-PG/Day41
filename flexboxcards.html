Grave yard

A graveyard, is a designated area where deceased individuals are buried, and it often serves as a place for remembrance, reflection, and honoring the departed.

Under taker

An undertaker is a professional in the funeral industry responsible for managing and arranging funeral services, including the preparation and burial of the deceased.

Black magic

Black magic involves the use of supernatural powers or rituals for malevolent purposes, often associated with harmful spells, curses, or manipulation of mystical forces.

Voodoo doll

A voodoo doll, a conduit of mystical influence, symbolizes the intricate connection between the physical and the spiritual in occult practices.

Cemetry

A cemetery, where silent memories rest beneath solemn stones, telling tales of lives once lived.
